My Story

From a young age, I've always been fascinated with the intersection between storytelling and digital experiences. One of my most cherished memories is playing the level creator mode in Lego Indiana Jones 2 and Super Mario Maker with my brother. My favourite part was always coming up with a story to accompany the puzzles we made.

Nowadays, I use my passion for storytelling to fuel all of my work. Whether it is through little details in the UI of a game, or a cinematic motion graphics project, I always remember that story is key.

Currently I'm 3rd year student studying a joint major with the School of Interactive Art and Technology and Beedie School of Business at Simon Fraser University.

I also happen to be a published author, with my charity work for Crohn's and Colitis Canada being featured on CBC news, and having the chance to have a comic displayed at the United Nations' Responsible Use of AI in the Military Summit in Korea this past year.

When I'm not designing or writing, you can find me struggling to learn TFT, playing games with friends, or learning songs with no bar chords on the guitar!
